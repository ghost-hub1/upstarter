Upstart 2022 Summer Celebrations

3 minute read
September 14, 2022
  • Home
  • Upstart Updates
  • Company Culture

2022 is proving to be quite a bit more challenging than any of us expected. In these moments, we believe getting together is important—to connect in person, recognize the great work we have done together and appreciate the time spent with one another.

After a two-year hiatus of in-person summer festivities, Upstart was excited to host the 2022 Summer Celebrations for Upstarters and their families!

In Columbus, Ohio, Upstarters had a picnic-style buffet, spent time exploring the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium, and captured the day with photobooth pictures and caricatures!

In San Mateo, California, Upstarters enjoyed Barbeque at a park by the Bay and played an assortment of carnival games and lawn games. The face painter and balloon artist were a hit with our families!

In Austin, Texas, Upstarters celebrated with a taco bar, beverages, lawn games, watching armadillo races, face painting, and enjoyed the sounds of their co-workers belting out their favorite karaoke songs.

Upstart hosted a contest for our community of Upstarters to send in their favorite photos from the 2022 Summer Celebrations.
